Results-driven, custom research

We design rigorous, cost-effective research that delivers actionable insights. By combining qualitative and quantitative methods, we assess not just whether a program works—but how, why, and where it works best. Our tailored studies help agencies make smarter decisions, maximize impact, and ensure taxpayer dollars are spent wisely.

Expert-led, impact-focused survey services

With over 40 years of experience, we deliver large-scale, complex surveys backed by deep methodological, statistical, and domain expertise. Our nationwide network of specialists spans health, education, energy, environment, transportation, and more—ensuring policy-relevant insights grounded in real-world understanding. Certified to ISO 20252:2020, we bring transparency, consistency, and rigor to every project—helping agencies make data-driven decisions that serve the public effectively.

Program evaluation that drives performance and accountability

We help agencies assess what works, what doesn’t, and how to improve—so programs deliver real value. From early-stage assessments to long-term evaluations, ICF applies rigorous, data-driven methods to measure impact, identify inefficiencies, and strengthen outcomes. Our work supports smarter decisions, better use of taxpayer dollars, and programs that perform.
